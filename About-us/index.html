About Us

Welcome to Ridetheflo (RTF) -

At (RTF), we believe that the magic behind every hit song is more than just the artist—it’s the entire ecosystem that brings it to life. From producers and sound engineers to publicists and marketers, the real heartbeat of the music industry beats behind the scenes. We’re here to celebrate these unsung heroes and showcase their invaluable contributions to Africa's vibrant music scene.

RTF isn’t just a brand; it’s a movement—one that documents, highlights, and honors the hard work that fuels the glitz and glamour of the entertainment industry.

Our Story: More Than Just Music

At RTF, we’re more than just an urban media and entertainment brand. We’re the voice of the behind-the-scenes players who make the music happen. Our Mission

Our mission is clear: to bolster the ever-growing music and entertainment industry in Nigeria and beyond. Through exclusive content and in-depth coverage, we’re pushing the boundaries of how the African music scene is understood. By documenting both the successes and the struggles of the industry's movers and shakers, RTF is here to give flowers to everyone—because every success story has a team behind it.
